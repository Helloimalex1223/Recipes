Lasagna

Description

Layers of perfectly cooked lasagna noodles, rich and savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella come together in this timeless Italian dish. Baked to perfection, the result is a deliciously hearty and comforting lasagna that satisfies every craving for a hearty, flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato sauce
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning (oregano, basil, thyme)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)
  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat.
  3. Add finely chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Saut√© until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, and tomato paste. Season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with an egg. Mix well.
  6. Preheat the oven to the temperature specified on the lasagna noodle package.
  7. Assemble the lasagna layers in a baking dish: Start with a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles, a layer of ricotta mixture, and a layer of shredded mozzarella. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of mozzarella on top.
  8. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top of the final layer.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the lasagna is heated through (typically 25-30 minutes).
  10. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  11. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if desired, then slice and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
