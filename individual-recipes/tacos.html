Tacos

Description

Savor the vibrant flavors of Mexico with these delicious homemade tacos. Seasoned and perfectly grilled or pan-fried protein, whether it's savory ground beef, chicken, or flavorful black beans, is nestled in warm corn tortillas. Top it off with a medley of fresh ingredients like crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, shredded cheese, and a dollop of zesty salsa. These tacos are a fiesta of taste and texture that will satisfy your craving for a quick and tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken (for protein)
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional, for garnish)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook the protein (ground beef or chicken) in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked.
  2. Drain any excess fat and add taco seasoning, following the package instructions. Mix well and simmer until the flavors meld.
  3. While the protein is cooking, warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos: Spoon the seasoned protein onto the center of each tortilla.
  5. Add shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese on top of the protein.
  6. Top each taco with a dollop of salsa, and if desired, a spoonful of sour cream.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro if you like, and serve the tacos with lime wedges on the side.
  8. Enjoy these flavorful tacos immediately, with your favorite Mexican beverages on standby!
