Guacamole

Description

Creamy and flavorful, this guacamole recipe is a quick and easy way to enjoy the deliciousness of ripe avocados. With a perfect balance of fresh ingredients, it's the ideal dip for tortilla chips or a tasty topping for tacos and nachos.

Ingredients

  • Ripe avocados
  • Red onion, finely chopped
  • Tomato, diced
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional for heat)
  • Lime juice
  • Garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the ripe avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocados with a fork or potato masher until smooth or slightly chunky, depending on your preference.
  3. Add finely chopped red onion, diced tomato, chopped fresh cilantro, and minced jalapeño (if using) to the mashed avocados.
  4. Squeeze lime juice over the mixture to add a bright, citrusy flavor.
  5. Add minced garlic, salt, and pepper to taste. Mix all ingredients until well combined.
  6. Adjust the seasoning if needed, and refrigerate the guacamole for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  7. Remove from the refrigerator, give it a final stir, and serve the guacamole with your favorite tortilla chips or as a topping for your favorite dishes.
